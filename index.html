Personalized Connections

Our Goal

Increasing equity in the opportunities students have to interact with their intended field of work before entering the workforce. We live in a world where having connections helps people get ahead, and it isn't fair that some students get left behind because they don't have access to professionals' advice because they weren't born with the connections hat others were born with. We will bridge this gap by connecting students with professionals in their fields of interest for them to understand their intended careers and form connections that help you develop professionally.
